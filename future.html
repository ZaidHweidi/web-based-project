What is the Future Simple?

The Future Simple tense is used to describe actions or events that will happen in the future. It is commonly formed using the auxiliary verb "will" or the phrase "going to."

  • Decisions made at the moment: I will call you later.
  • Predictions: It will rain tomorrow.
  • Plans: She is going to visit her grandmother this weekend.
